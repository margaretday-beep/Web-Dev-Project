Welcome to Chic Cashflows!

This website is here to guide young women (you!) through the financial struggles that face us in the 21st century. Whether you need assistance with budgeting, taxes, investing, or choosing the best credit card for your situation, Chic Cashflows is here to help.

Women face many issues in personal finance today, including:

  • Gender pay gap
  • Less confidence and delayed retirement planning
  • Lower levels of investing (when compared to men)
